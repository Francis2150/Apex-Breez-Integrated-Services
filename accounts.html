Our Process

How We Set Up Your System

From initial assessment to post-deployment support, we handle everything.

1. Needs Assessment

We evaluate your business processes and recommend the most suitable accounting software based on your structure and compliance requirements.

2. System Design & Setup

We design your chart of accounts and configure ledgers, tax parameters, and reports tailored to your specific operations.

3. Data Migration

We assist in importing your previous financial data and setting opening balances for a smooth, loss-free transition.

4. User Training

Our team provides hands-on training to your staff on how to effectively use the system for day-to-day financial operations.

5. Technical Support & Maintenance

We provide ongoing after-deployment support including software updates, backup assistance, and quick issue resolution.
