What We Offer

Types of Business Registration

We support clients with major business entity types recognized under Ghana's registration framework.

Sole Proprietorship

The simplest business structure — ideal for individuals starting a small business. You have full control over decisions and profits.

  • Single owner, full control
  • Easy to register & maintain
  • Lower registration fees

Partnership

A formal agreement between two or more persons to run a business together. Partners share profits, losses, and liabilities.

  • Two or more partners
  • Partnership agreement required
  • Shared responsibilities & profits

Limited Liability Company (LLC)

The most popular business structure — separates your personal assets from business liabilities. Ideal for growing businesses.

  • Limited personal liability
  • Requires minimum 2 directors
  • Professional credibility

NGO / Non-Profit

Register your non-profit organization to operate legally, access funding, and make a recognized impact in Ghana.

  • RGD registration
  • Constitution & by-laws drafting
  • Eligible for donor funding
Simple Process

How Business Registration Works

We handle the paperwork — you focus on building your business.

1. Consultation

Contact us via phone, WhatsApp, or email. We'll understand your business needs and recommend the right structure.

2. Document Collection

We'll request the required documents — ID, photos, address details, and business information.

3. Processing

We submit all documents to RGD, GRA, and relevant authorities. You'll receive regular status updates.

4. Delivery

You receive your certificate of registration, TIN, and all official documents — ready to operate legally.

What You Need

Requirements for Registration

  • Valid national ID (Ghana Card, Passport, or Voter's ID)
  • Two (2) passport-size photographs
  • Digital address of business location
  • Description of business activities
  • Tax Identification Number (TIN) for all directors
  • Proposed business name(s) — 2-3 options
What's Included

What You Receive

  • Certificate of Incorporation / Registration
  • Certificate to Commence Business
  • Tax Identification Number (TIN)
  • GRA Registration & VAT Setup (if applicable)
  • Company Regulations / Constitution
  • Official company stamp (for LLCs)
