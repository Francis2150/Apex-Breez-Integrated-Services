  • Partnership Registration

    Involves the creation of a formal agreement between two or more persons to run a business together. All partners are jointly responsible for liabilities and profits.

    Partnership agreements must be submitted during registration and should outline roles, profit sharing, and dispute resolution mechanisms.

  • Requirements for Business Registration

    Applicants are required to provide a valid national ID, passport-size photographs, digital address, and details of business location and activity.

    Additionally, registered tax identification numbers (TINs) of all owners or directors are mandatory.
