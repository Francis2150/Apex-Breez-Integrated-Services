Last Updated: January 2025

At Apex Breez Integrated Services, we strive to deliver exceptional business registration, website development, and accounting services. Due to the nature of our services, our refund policy is structured as follows:

1. Business Registration Services

Once a business registration process has commenced with the Registrar General's Department (RGD) or Ghana Revenue Authority (GRA), government fees and our service charges are strictly non-refundable. We cannot cancel a registration once it has been submitted to the authorities.

If a request is cancelled before we submit your details to the government, a refund may be issued minus any administrative or processing fees incurred.

2. Website Development Services

  • Before Project Commencement: If you cancel before work begins, you are entitled to a full refund.
  • During Development: Refunds are calculated based on the percentage of the project completed. Work already completed will be billed at the agreed hourly or project rate.
  • After Delivery: No refunds are provided once the final website has been delivered, approved, and deployed.

Domain registration and hosting fees are non-refundable once the domain has been purchased.

3. Accounting System Setup

Due to the consulting and technical nature of software setup and data migration, service fees for accounting system setups are non-refundable once the configuration process has started.

4. How to Request a Refund

To request a refund, please contact us at:

  • Email: franciskwadzoamuzu@gmail.com
  • Phone/WhatsApp: 055-732-7096

Refund requests will be reviewed within 5 business days. Approved refunds will be processed back to the original payment method within 10-14 business days.
